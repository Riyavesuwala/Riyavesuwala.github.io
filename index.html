Innovative Projects

WiseAid – AI Collaboration Platform

The Vision: To bridge the gap between complex AI research and everyday utility. In this project, I moved beyond standard development by experimenting with LLaMA models and Hugging Face models.

By integrating pre-built intelligence with a custom-built collaboration framework, I created a system that provides real-time audio transcription and automated summaries. This isn't just a chat tool; it’s a fresh approach to helpful, AI-driven societal tools.

Blazor • ASP.NET Core • LLaMA

Sarvameet – Assistive Communication System

The Impact: A mission-driven project aimed at fostering an inclusive society. This team-led innovation provides a vital communication bridge for deaf and mute individuals through real-time interaction translation.

Our idea gained massive recognition, securing 3rd Place at IIT Roorkee (Ideastorm) and being showcased at IIT Bombay’s e-Yantra. Furthermore, the project was honored with a Certificate of Appreciation from Parul University, proving that technology, when applied with empathy, can dismantle long-standing social barriers.

Accessibility • AI • Social Innovation • Award Winning

Government Grievance Redressal System

The Solution: A comprehensive administrative ecosystem designed by our team to ensure public accountability through digital transparency. This system automates the full lifecycle of a citizen's complaint—from submission to final resolution.

We architected a structured workflow that routes grievances to appropriate departments with built-in escalation frameworks. Working as a team on this project allowed us to handle complex data logic and create a robust system that improves government-citizen interactions.

Enterprise Java • SQL Server • Team Collaboration

Student Attendance App

The Prototype: Developed as a collaborative final semester project, this app addresses the inefficiency of manual classroom management. Our team identified the "pain point" of tedious attendance tracking and built a mobile-first solution.

By utilizing Firebase for real-time synchronization, we ensured the app provides instant updates and secure data storage. It is a testament to how a focused team effort can turn a small idea into a functional, helpful tool for academic environments.

Kotlin • Firebase • Team Innovation
